DOWN WITH BRIGADEIRO!

It's true that our beloved Brigadeiro is the most popular brazilian dessert.

But as a Brazilian, I just can't agree with it's popularity when we can do so much better.

Today I'm going to show you an underrated dessert that is not only Brazilian (ok, one might say that the Portuguese invented it, but we just improved the Quindim by replacing expensive almonds with coconut. Fair enough!) but is also better than Brigadeiro!

Behold... The Quindim!

The recipe is quite simple, you'll just need a lot of egg yolks.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 15 egg yolks
  • 1 ½ cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons butter (melted)
  • 100g coconut
  • pinch of salt

PREPARE:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and grease a muffin tin;
  2. Stir together sugar, coconut, melted butter, and salt in a medium bowl. Gradually add egg yolks, stirring well after each addition;
  3. Fill the muffin tin with coconut in the bottom, then stir in the mix from step 2;
  4. In a large roasting pan, add hot water and place the muffin tin with the quindim mix, carefully;
  5. Bake for 40 minutes;
  6. Carefully remove if from the oven and serve at room temperature, or cool the quindins in the fridge.

Bom apetite!
